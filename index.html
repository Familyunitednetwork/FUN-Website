    About Us

    The Family Union Network (FUN) is a dedicated community organization committed to serving the Arab and Muslim community. Our primary goal is to foster a strong sense of community and provide comprehensive support to families through a variety of initiatives:

    • Social Gatherings: We organize regular social events to promote community cohesion and offer a welcoming environment for our sons and daughters to connect and thrive.
    • Educational and Ethical Sessions: We offer purposeful educational and ethical sessions designed for the entire family, with a special focus on boys and girls, to promote growth and development.
    • Community Support: We provide essential services and support to all members of the families within our community, ensuring everyone has the resources they need.
    • Community Projects: We identify and implement essential community projects that meet the unmet needs of Arab and Muslim families, ensuring they have access to vital social services.
    • Celebrations and Events: We celebrate and magnify Muslim rituals and holidays through special gatherings and events, fostering a sense of unity and cultural pride.

    Soccer

    The Egyptian Canadian Cultural Society of BC (ECCS BC) is Proud to present the Egyptian Soccer Club, ESC, as part of its activities. EAS is all run by Volunteers. The Club is a place for all Egyptians and Arab residents of BC. Every Soccer lover can come join and enjoy, practice and mock games. All Ages from 14 and up are welcome. ECCS BC uses turf or grass fields where available, and indoors gym during fall and winter. Come and register with us and join the fun.
